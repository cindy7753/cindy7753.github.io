About me

Project

Here are some projects that I've worked on for the past year.

Usability Evaluation

My team and I evaluated the functionality and usability of a mobile app- Ramblehook. We implemented a series of UX research methods such as, survey, compartive analysis, heuristic evaluation, and usability testing. We discover some critical findings that we believe would benefit Ramblehook in improving its usability.

Graphic Design

In this course, I demonstrated my graphic design skills in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Indesign. I worked on mutiple pieces that shows my creativity and sense of aesthetic.

Climate Change Informatics

The project aim is to provide possible solution to build the gap between climate science data & usable science data. We proposed a web-based platform, that allows non-climate people to explore usable climate resouces, and a location for them to dicuss and share knowledge of climate data/models with climate experts.

Interaction Design

In this individual project, I proposed an integrated online wishlist system that manage shopper's wishlist across different shopping websites. A virtual fitting room is also included to view how well the clothings fit. Moreover, shoppers are allowed to send these images and share them with friends and family.

Contextual Inquiry

In this team project, my team and I examined the internal workflow of our client by using a series of contexual inquiry skills such as interview, observation, work models, and affinity wall. We reveal some critical points of breakdown in their workflow, and give constructive recommendations.

Babycenter.com

In this project, I worked as an research assistant to compare cultural differences in parenting boards. Our team examine countries across US, Canada, India, UK, and China to identify their differences in social norms, and the type of support people seek on the boards.
